What is Sous Vide?

Sous Vide is a cooking technique that involves precision water temperature to ensure that food is cooked to exact tastes, without ever being overdone. The results of this method bring the juiciest steaks, the flakiest fish, and the happiest of friends and family.

Michael Paratore
